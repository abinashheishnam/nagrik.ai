About Portal

The Manipur Grievance Portal (Praja.ai) is an advanced AI-powered 911-style reporting system designed to streamline citizen-government communication. Launched by the Government of Manipur, it leverages Artificial Intelligence to triage, categorize, and prioritize grievances in real-time, ensuring rapid response to critical infrastructure and public service issues.

Version 2.4.0 (Stable) • Built with ❤️ by Praja.ai Team
Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
❓ How does the AI Scoring work?
Our NLP engine analyzes the complaint text for keywords indicating urgency (e.g., "danger", "blocked", "fire"). It combines this with location data and category severity to assign a Risk Factor (0-100%).
❓ How do I assign a field officer?
Go to the detail view of any grievance. Currently, assignment is handled automatically based on department, but manual override will be available in the Workforce module soon.
❓ Is the location data accurate?
We use browser-based geolocation (high accuracy) combined with address reverse-geocoding. Users can also manually correct their address if the GPS drift is high.
